CatalogWatcher Pro

Your Ultimate IMVU Product Tracking Discord Bot

What is CatalogWatcher Pro?

CatalogWatcher Pro is a powerful Discord bot designed to track new product drops from your favorite IMVU creators. Built with a Python backend and Discord API, it delivers real-time notifications with a touch of humor, ensuring you never miss a new item!

Real-Time Tracking

Monitors IMVU creator catalogs every 10 minutes to notify you instantly when new products are added.

Custom Alerts

Configure notifications for specific creators, channels, or roles, and get direct messages for your favorite creators.

Meme-Powered

Adds a fun twist with 100+ random jokes included in product updates, keeping your community entertained.

Key Features

IMVU Catalog Scraping

Uses aiohttp and BeautifulSoup to scrape IMVU product pages, extracting details like price, status, and audience (GA/AP).

Rich Discord Embeds

Sends visually appealing embeds with product images, prices, and a "Buy Now" button for quick purchases.

Flexible Configuration

Manage creators, channels, and roles with commands like /add_creator, /set_notify_channel, and more.

Personalized DM Alerts

Use /watch_creator to receive direct messages for new drops from your favorite creators.

Bot Commands

  • /add_creator: Add a creator to track with their IMVU catalog URL.
  • /remove_creator: Stop tracking a creator.
  • /list_creators: View all tracked creators.
  • /check_now: Force an immediate product scan.
  • /set_notify_channel: Set the default channel for notifications.
  • /watch_creator: Get DMs for a creator's new drops.
  • /drop_stats: See how many drops a creator has logged.
  • /help: View all available commands.
